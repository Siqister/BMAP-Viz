How Do Bostonians Get to Work?

Hover over the graphic to learn how people in different parts of the city get to work, and how that compares to the city overall.

What are those rectangles?

Each row of rectangles represents a single census tract. Within this row, each rectangle represents the number of workers commuting by different modes.

What does the map show?

In different areas of Boston, do people proportionally take transit/drive/walk etc. more than the city at large? Red means more the city average, blue represents below city average, and white is in the middle.

Where is this data from?

American Community Survey 2013 5-year estimate
